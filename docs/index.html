Eric Li 李宗旭

Hi! I am Zongxu, a first-year Ph.D. student in Statistics at Shanghai University of Finance and Economics (SUFE). I am advised by Fan Zhou.

My research interests focus on machine learning theory and large language models, especially reasoning, reinforcement learning from human feedback (RLHF), stats-powered AI, StatRL, CausalRL, and off-policy evaluation (OPE). I am particularly interested in how statistical theory can provide reliable evaluation, uncertainty quantification, and principled learning objectives for modern AI systems. My long-term goal is to develop methods that connect statistics, reinforcement learning, and causal inference for trustworthy decision-making.

Before SUFE, I received an M.A. in Economics from Shandong University, where I was fortunate to be advised by Youquan Pei and Yanlai Chen. I received my B.A. in Economics from Qingdao University.
